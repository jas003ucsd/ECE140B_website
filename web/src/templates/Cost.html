Cost

We determined that the cost to produce this product will be approximately $56 total. The following is a breakdown of by component per unit:
-camera $17
-Raspberry pi zero $15
-Contact Sensor $4
-Housing for componets $5
-labor Cost $15
We found this using consumer pricing for our components. So we believe our margins will be better than shown

The Fixed cost for product is server hosting at $50/month and Clarifai API at $30/month. These are the lower tiers that are aim at smaller business that are trying to ramp up.

Revenue

We hope to sell 5,000 units the firsts year. Pricing the product at 75 per unit will cover the cost of product and maintaining fix cost. This would bring yearly profit to approx $100,000 for first year profit.

We discussed also including adds in the app and charging to turn them off. this would still be under consideration. Another revenue string would be sell data on user food usage and habits. We believe this could be unethical. The last possible revenue stream was starting a service that you could use minimum inventory and auto order essential groceries for a small service fee. This is a feature we would like to add after the MVP process.
