Our Grocery store tracker helps shoppers or home cooks who want to make sure they do not buy un-need groceries or verify they have the required groceries at their home by automating the inventory process and creating a database of items on hand unlike other apps that just track what you bought. The Key Value Proposition of the Grocery Tracker is a seamless tracking of groceries going into and out of the user cabinet or fridge. The tracker is easy installed and hidden out of sight and will automate the tracking of the groceries. The user can easily access what is in the cabinet with an app or webpage.

This will allow for the user to never take inventory of what is in the cabinets and provide peace of mind when at the store that item they need is waiting for them at home. This will allow for the user to know exactly when an item has been placed in the cabinet.

Our product will allow for a completely automated the inventory of the food in your home. This would be an improvement over manually inventorying. Since manually would be time-consuming to perform and possible error. Also, this would be an improvement over apps that scan receipts since would track when you are use food and not just buy food. User would see the following benefits:
  • Performance: never have to check if you have an item since it is in app.
  • Risk Reduction: never buy something at the store that you have at home.
  • Convenience: any app with get you all the data you need.
  • Save money: less money spend on items you already own or expense from multiple trips to the store.
